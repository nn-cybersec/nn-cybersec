Privacy Policy (Personal Data Processing)

Publication date: 20 Oct 2025

1. General Provisions

This Policy defines the procedure and conditions for processing personal data (PD) of participants of the “International Young Scientists School ‘Artificial Intelligence & Cybersecurity’” (the “Event”). It applies to all PD collected via the registration form and other communication channels within the preparation and conduct of the Event.

2. Personal Data Controller

Controller: North-Caucasus Federal University (NCFU)
Address: 1 Pushkina St., 355017, Stavropol, Russia • TIN/OGRN: 2635014955 / 1022601961580
PD inquiries: nn-cybersec@mail.ru

3. Scope of Data Processed

We process the data you provide in the registration form: last name, first name, patronymic (if any), date of birth, organization, position, city, and e-mail. Other data may be processed only if provided voluntarily and for the stated purposes.

4. Purposes and Legal Grounds

  • organization and conduct of the Event; communication with participants;
  • issuing passes/certificates; sending programs and notifications;
  • statistical and reporting purposes; archival storage.

Legal grounds: your consent to PD processing (Art. 6 of Federal Law No. 152-FZ) and/or necessity to conclude and perform an agreement on participation.

5. Processing Actions

Collection, recording, systematization, storage, updating, use, transfer under instruction, anonymization, blocking, deletion, and destruction of PD.

6. Processors and Third-Party Transfers

The registration and storage of applications use Yandex Forms (Yandex acts as a processor on behalf of the Controller). Additional processors may include the website hosting provider and e-mail services for notifications. Transfers are based on contracts/public terms ensuring PD protection requirements.

7. Storage Location and Localization

Initial recording and storage of PD of Russian citizens are carried out within the territory of the Russian Federation (Art. 18.1 of Federal Law No. 152-FZ).

8. Retention Period

Until the purposes are achieved and/or for 3 years after the Event ends, or until consent is withdrawn, unless a longer period is required by law.

9. Data Subject Rights

You have the right to obtain information about processing, request rectification, blocking or deletion of PD, object to processing, and withdraw your consent. Requests may be sent to the Controller’s postal address or by e-mail at nn-cybersec@mail.ru.

10. Withdrawal of Consent

You may withdraw consent at any time by sending an e-mail titled “Withdrawal of consent to PD processing” to nn-cybersec@mail.ru. Upon receipt, processing will cease except where continued processing is permitted by law.

11. Security Measures

The Controller applies legal, organizational, and technical measures to protect PD against unlawful access, destruction, modification, blocking, copying, and dissemination, including access control, processing logs, and backup procedures.

12. Cookies and Logs

The website may use cookies and web server technical logs solely to ensure website functionality and for aggregated statistics. Marketing trackers are not used without separate consent.

13. Changes to this Policy

The current version of this Policy is available on this page. In case of significant changes, the publication date will be updated.
